eLearn Economics


eLearn Economics - Online Learning

HOME - introduction, number of online tests completed by students, subscription options and demo access.

SUBSCRIPTIONS - Both individual students and schools can purchase a yearly subscription through PayPal.

LOGIN - Fully subscribed students and guest members access their respective user features.

KEYNOTES - each of the 110 topics has it's own 'Key Notes' offering a written overview and supportive diagrams.

MULTI-CHOICE - this section tests the students knowledge and records their progress througout the year.

PROGRESS PAGE - displays a colour coded test history for instant feedback of students monthly progress.

LEADER-BOARD - shows students results in relation to other students from both their own school and nationally.

Scope:

Rennie Resources Ltd originally published paper based economics resources for secondary schools in New Zealand. We were tasked with converting these resources into an online platform that would allow for immediate updates and additions, keeping content fresh and relevant. The site also allows for students to complete online tests and track their results through the year anabling them to monitor their progress and better prepare for exams.

Responsive:

eLearn Economics is primarily focused on students and it was important they could access and use the site via mobile devices as well as a desktop environment in classrooms.

Technologies used:

HTML5, CSS3 (Bootstrap), jQuery, PHP (Codeigniter framework), MySql database.
