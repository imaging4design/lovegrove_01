La Cigale - French Markets

HOME - links to the four main site categories, market, bistro, shop, functions and owners introduction.

QUICK LINKS - Navigation Menu, site search for recipes and user photo upload gallery.

Scope:

A rebuild of the clients existing website, updating the design and technology to a new fresh and modern look, whilst enabling the client to administer their own content management. Client required an easy to use system that would cater for non-technical staff to maintain.

Responsive:

A key requirement was mobile accessibility, as analytical data illustrated an increasing amount of traffic to the site was from mobile devices.

Technologies used:

HTML5, CSS3 (Bootstrap), jQuery, Wordpress, MySql database.
