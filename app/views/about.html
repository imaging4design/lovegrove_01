Sporting Background - Javelin throw

Way back at high school I started what was to be a fifteen year career in track and field athletics as a javelin thrower. After some early sucesses at school level I was fortunate to go on and represent New Zealand at Olympic, Commonwealth and World Championship level. In 1986, I was selected for the XIII Commonwealth Games in Edinburgh, Scotland and as an 18 year old won a bronze medal – one of my most enduring memories. Other career highlights included two World Junior javelin records, two Olympics Games, bronze medals at the World Junior Championships and Commonwealth Games 1986, 1990 and 1994 and 11 national javelin records culminating in 88.20m in Oslo Norway 1996.
