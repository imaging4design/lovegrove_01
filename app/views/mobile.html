Are you ready for the mobile world?

As the uptake of mobile devices like smartphones and tablets continues to rise, tasks that were once the domain of desktops (i.e. web browsing) are now being performed with ease and sometimes preference on these smaller portable devices. And it's not just surfing the web, it's everything from browsing social media platforms, checking emails, booking reservations to online shopping. Mobile internet usage is steadily increasing and it's very important that your website is mobile friendly and able to engage your users' on their preferred devices. In fact, think 'mobile first' - it's here and here to stay!
