Logo design


What is a logo?

A logo is used to 'identify'. It is only one aspect of a company's brand or entity and its colours, fonts, symbols and shape are usually very different from that of its market competitors. An effective logo must me immediately recognisable, instantiate trust and credibility and display a high level of professionalism.

The logo design process

People often ask "what’s so difficult about creating a good logo"? They’re small, they look easy to design, so what's the problem? Well, when you only see the final result of the designer’s work, the logo itself can look as though it was a simple straight forward task, but in reality it’s not. A logo takes a lot of thought and creativity, and requires many elements to come together to create a good one.

An effective logo design should encompass 5 key criteria. It should be:

  • Simple
  • Memorable
  • Timeless
  • Versatile
  • Appropriate
